Guides on making courses

Challenges

While showing your student every single line of code they should write leads to easy tutorials, it won't help students to learn the fundamental concepts.

A course should teach a student concepts, and then apply those concepts through code. Otherwise your students may just be copy pasting stuff without having any idea how it works.

A challenge should have the student take some concepts they've already learned (and seen or used at least once in code) and build a predefined feature or idea using those concepts.

Make sure the challenge only expects the student to take existsing concepts and build on them.

Predefined means that you give some sort of definition for what you are expecting the student to do.
